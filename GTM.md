# GTM & Growth Strategy — Credex AI Spend Audit

## 1. Value Proposition
"Find hidden AI subscription waste in under 2 minutes."
- **Primary Benefit**: Cost reduction (average 24% found).
- **Secondary Benefit**: Stack optimization & simplified procurement.

## 2. Acquisition Channels

### Viral Loop (Primary)
- **Shareable Reports**: Every audit generates a public URL with a visual "Savings Card".
- **Social Proof**: OG images display the potential savings (e.g., "I just saved $430/mo using Credex AI Audit").
- **Platform**: LinkedIn and X (Twitter) are primary targets for founders.

### Content Marketing
- **SaaS Benchmarking Reports**: Quarterly data on "The State of AI Spend in Startups" using anonymized audit data.
- **Comparison Guides**: "ChatGPT vs. Claude vs. Gemini: Which one should your team consolidate to?"

### Communities
- **Product Hunt**: Launching the free tool as a "Product of the Day".
- **Hacker News**: Showcasing the technical audit engine logic.
- **Indie Hackers**: Targeting solo-founders with high tool density.

## 3. Funnel Optimization

1. **Visit**: Landing page with high-impact hero.
2. **Engage**: Zero-friction tool entry form (no signup required).
3. **Capture**: Gated results reveal via email.
4. **Convert**: "Book a Consultation" CTA for deeper stack optimization.
5. **Advocate**: "Share your results" loop.

## 4. Positioning
- **Not a calculator**: It's a forensic audit tool.
- **Proof of Expertise**: Demonstrates that Credex understands the modern AI stack better than generic finance tools.
