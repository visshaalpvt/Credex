# Unit Economics — Credex AI Spend Audit

This document outlines the hypothetical unit economics of the tool as a lead generation engine.

## 1. Costs (CAC)
- **Development**: ~$5k (internal builder cost).
- **Hosting**: ~$20/mo (Vercel + Supabase).
- **AI Credits**: ~$0.02 per audit (Claude 3 Haiku).
- **Paid Distribution**: $2.50 per click (LinkedIn Ads).
- **Blended CAC**: Assuming 10% conversion from visit to lead, CAC = **$25.00**.

## 2. Revenue Potential (LTV)
- **Consulting Upsell**: $2,500 - $10,000 per engagement.
- **Conversion Rate**: 2% of audit leads book a paid engagement.
- **LTV Calculation**: (0.02 * $5,000) = **$100.00 per lead**.

## 3. Economics Summary
- **Lead Value**: $100.00
- **Lead Cost**: $25.00
- **ROI**: 4x (300%)

## 4. Scaling Assumptions
As the tool gains viral traction (K-factor > 0.3), the organic CAC drops significantly, increasing the ROI to 10x+.
