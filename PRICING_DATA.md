Where official pricing is "Contact Sales", the audit engine uses market benchmarks:
- **ChatGPT Enterprise**: ~$60/user/month
- **Claude Enterprise**: ~$30-45/user/month (negotiable)
- **Jasper Business**: ~$150/user/month
- **ElevenLabs Enterprise**: Benchmark of $99+ based on scale
