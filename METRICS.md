# Metrics & KPIs — Credex AI Spend Audit

We track the following metrics to measure the success of the tool as a lead magnet and viral product.

## 1. Top of Funnel
- **Unique Visitors**: Total visitors to the landing page.
- **Form Completion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who add at least 1 tool.
- **Audit Generation Rate**: Percentage of users who click "Generate Audit".

## 2. Lead Conversion
- **Email Capture Rate**: Percentage of users who provide an email to unlock the full report.
- **Qualified Lead Rate**: Emails from domains associated with startups (excluding gmail/outlook).
- **Consultation CTR**: Percentage of users who click "Book a Consultation" on the results page.

## 3. Viral Metrics
- **Share Rate**: Percentage of users who click "Generate Share Link".
- **K-Factor**: Number of new visitors brought in by each shared report.
- **Social Mention Reach**: Total impressions on X/LinkedIn from shared OG images.

## 4. Product Impact
- **Average Savings Identified**: Total potential savings found per user.
- **Waste Score Distribution**: Percentage of users in High/Medium/Low waste brackets.
- **Most Duplicate Tools**: Tracking which tools are most frequently flagged for consolidation.

## 5. Technical Performance
- **Lighthouse Score**: Maintaining 90+ across Performance, Accessibility, and SEO.
- **Page Load Time**: Target < 1.2s for first contentful paint.
